RULE 11 — CA TIER SYSTEM
Tier 1 — Pure Subject Tag
Constitutional articles, historical events, scientific laws, fixed geographic facts — NO CA- prefix
Tier 2 — CA- [Subject]
Budget figures → CA- Economics · Indices/Reports → CA- Economics or CA- Environment & Ecology · New species → CA- Environment & Ecology · Space missions → CA- Science & Tech · New waterways → CA- Indian Geography · GS2 welfare schemes → CA- Public Administration
Tier 3 — Current Affairs
MOUs, Military exercises, Sports, Books & Authors, Key Appointments, Awards, Summits, Obituaries, Defence deals, Scheme name launches (no deeper subject)
Tier 4 — General Knowledge
NEVER manually assign. System auto-detect fallback only.
